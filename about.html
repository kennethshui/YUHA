Who We Are

The Yale Undergraduate Hedge Fund Association (YUHA) serves as an interactive
and inclusive platform to connect students and alumni with a shared interest in finance.

Structured into two parts, YUHA aims to strengthen students' investing prowess by
learning from an educational curriculum and the experiences of Yale alumni who are
involved in the sector. Members will have the opportunity to apply their gained
knowledge in a quantitative aspect, using a stock simulation to emulate
hedge fund investment approaches and engage in a stock-pitch competition.

YUHA is the catalyst for members to gain and solidify an interest in a career in finance.
